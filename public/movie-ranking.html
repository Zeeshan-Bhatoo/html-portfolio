The Best Movies According To Zeeshan

My Top 3 Picks So Far.

The Hobit

A classic fantastic type of movie which is a great escape from the modern tech world. Great storytelling paired with epic adventures and battles.

The Kingsman

Simply one of the best spy, secret service movie of the last decade. It constists of fancy suits and innovative weapons. To be honest the suits are enough to make me like the movie.

Cars

This one made my childhood! Who doesn't enjoy a nice race?
